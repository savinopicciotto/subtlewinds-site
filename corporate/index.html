The Experience

What your team
will experience

A live, in-person sound meditation is not background music or guided relaxation. It’s a structured practice that recalibrates attention and lowers the noise — literally and figuratively.

Acoustic Immersion

Singing bowls, chimes, gongs, and sustained tones fill the space. No recordings, no speakers. The sound moves through the room and through each person differently.

Nervous System Reset

Participants lie down or sit comfortably while layered sound frequencies guide them from active thinking into deep rest. Most people report effects lasting days, not hours.

Zero Effort Required

No experience needed. No awkward exercises. No talking circles. Your team shows up, lies down, and lets the sound do the work. It meets everyone where they are.

About

About the
facilitator

I’m Savino Picciotto — a meditation teacher and sound practitioner based in Brooklyn, NY. I’ve been facilitating sound meditations for over a decade, working across studios, venues, and corporate settings in New York City.

This isn’t a side gig. It’s a practice I’ve been developing since before “corporate wellness” was a line item. I bring the same depth to a boardroom session that I bring to an intimate gathering — because the practice doesn’t change based on who’s in the room.

I also build AI systems professionally, which means I understand the pace, pressure, and cognitive demands your team faces. This work is the antidote I wish more teams had access to.
