About

Different traditions,
same aim.

I'm a meditation teacher and contemplative practitioner — not a guru, not a monk, not a brand. I work with people who want a real practice without the jargon, the conversion energy, or the "just breathe" basics.

My path has moved through Tibetan Buddhist philosophy, sound meditation, earth-based ceremony, and consciousness practices rooted in intention and awareness. These aren't competing systems — they're different doors into the same room: waking up to what's here and learning to move through life with clarity and intention.

I also build AI products, make music, and produce sound-driven events — including the Harmonic Resonance Chamber ambient series at House of Yes. Practice isn't separate from life — it's the foundation everything else stands on.
