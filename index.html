About Subtle Winds

Built by builders, for builders.

Subtle Winds is an AI consulting firm founded by Savino Picciotto, a software engineer who's spent years at the intersection of AI and real-world business operations.

We built SubtleDoc because we saw construction companies drowning in paperwork — contracts in email, permits on desktops, invoices in filing cabinets. The tools that existed were either too expensive, too complicated, or not smart enough.

Our approach is different: we build AI systems that work the way you already work, not the other way around. Every deployment is customized to your business, your documents, and your team.
